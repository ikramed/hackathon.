Personality is the unique combination of characteristics, traits, and behaviors that make up who we are. Understanding personality can help us improve communication and build better relationships.

The Big Five personality traits are fundamental in understanding the diversity of personalities:

  • Openness: Willingness to try new things, creativity, and curiosity.
  • Conscientiousness: Responsibility, organization, and dependability.
  • Extraversion: Social behavior, assertiveness, and excitement-seeking.
  • Agreeableness: Cooperation, kindness, and empathy.
  • Neuroticism: Emotional stability, anxiety, and moodiness.
